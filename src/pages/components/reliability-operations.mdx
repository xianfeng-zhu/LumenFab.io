## What This Flow Does

Reliability and operations 关注的是 CPO 能否长期稳定运行。它跨越 laser、PIC、EIC、optical I/O、package、thermal design 和 system management。

典型问题包括：

- laser aging and burn-in。
- wavelength drift。
- thermal cycling。
- mechanical stress。
- connector and fiber handling。
- field replacement。
- fault isolation。
- link flap and system-level availability。

## Common Boundaries

- Reliability is not the same as initial performance.
- A lower-loss optical path can still be operationally fragile.
- CPO improves electrical reach but creates tighter packaging and maintenance constraints.

## Short Version

Reliability and operations explains what happens after the device leaves the lab. For CPO, this is central because optical, electrical and mechanical failures are harder to isolate once the system is densely integrated.
