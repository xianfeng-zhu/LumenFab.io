## What This Component Does

PIC, photonic integrated circuit, 是光引擎里的光路芯片。它让光在芯片内传播、分配、相位调制、强度调制、合波、分波、耦合和探测。

PIC 不等于激光器，也不等于单一材料平台。它可能基于 SiPh、InP PIC、SiN、TFLN，或不同平台的 hybrid / heterogeneous integration。

## Common Boundaries

- SiPh is a platform route, not a single device.
- MZM/MRM are modulator structures, not material platforms.
- SiN is low-loss passive, not native gain.
- TFLN is strong for modulation, not light generation.

## Short Version

PIC is the optical circuit layer. It should be explained through routing, modulation, coupling, loss and platform choice, not as a company category or a loose synonym for silicon photonics.
