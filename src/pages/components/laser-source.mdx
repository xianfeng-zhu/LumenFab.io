## What This Component Does

Light source 负责给光引擎提供可调制、可耦合、可长期工作的光。它可以是 DFB、EML、external CW laser、laser array，具体形态取决于架构。

在 CPO 语境里，激光源不只是“一个发光芯片”。它牵涉：

- raw materials and InP substrate。
- epitaxy and active region。
- laser cavity and feedback。
- chip-level test。
- burn-in and aging。
- coupling into PIC or package。
- thermal path and wavelength stability。

## Common Boundaries

- InP substrate is not the laser.
- QW/MQW is active-region detail, not substrate detail.
- DFB is a cavity/feedback structure, not a material platform.
- EML is a device integration form, not a raw material.

## Short Version

Light source is where material purity, InP substrate quality, epitaxy, gain physics, cavity design, thermal control and reliability all meet. This entrance covers both laser chips and external laser supply.
