## What This Component Does

EIC, electronic integrated circuit, 是光引擎里的电子侧。它连接 switch ASIC、driver、TIA、retimer/DSP、控制电路和电源网络。

在 CPO 中，EIC 和 PIC 的距离更近，电互连长度更短，但这也把热、封装、电源完整性和测试问题推到更高难度。

## Common Boundaries

- LPO/CPO are architecture and system-boundary questions, not modulator structures.
- Driver is not the same thing as modulator.
- TIA belongs to receiver electronics, not optical detection material.

## Short Version

EIC is the electrical control and readout side of the optical engine. It is where high-speed electrical loss, driver swing, receiver noise, power and heat meet the optical components.
