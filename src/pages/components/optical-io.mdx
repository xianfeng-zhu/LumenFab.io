## What This Component Does

Optical I/O 把芯片里的光接到芯片外。它通常包括 edge coupler、grating coupler、fiber array、lens、connector、光纤走线和相关的对准结构。

它值得单独作为入口，因为 CPO 的难点不只是把芯片放近，还包括让大量光通道稳定、低损耗、可装配、可测试地离开封装。

## Common Boundaries

- Optical I/O is not just packaging glue.
- Coupling loss is not only a PIC design issue.
- Fiber connector and fiber routing can dominate manufacturability and serviceability.

## Short Version

Optical I/O is the boundary where chip-scale optical modes become system-level fiber links. It belongs near packaging, but it deserves its own entrance.
