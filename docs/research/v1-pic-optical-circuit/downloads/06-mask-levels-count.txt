--- Typical Mask Levels in Silicon Photonics (imec iSiPP50G) ---

Silicon patterning:  3 mask levels (70nm, 160nm, 220nm etch)
Poly-Si layer:       1 mask level
Si implants:         8 mask levels (4x n-type, 4x p-type)
Ge module:           2 mask levels (Ge epitaxy + implants)
Contacts:            1 mask level (W plugs)
Heater metal:        1 mask level
Metal 1 (M1):        1 mask level
Metal 2 (M2):        1 mask level
Passivation:         1 mask level
Deep trench:         1 mask level (edge coupler facets)

Estimated total:     ~20 mask levels for full active platform

For Passives+ (no implants, no Ge):
  ~8-10 mask levels

Note: The exact count depends on whether you count each implant as a separate mask, and on the specific integration of optional modules (SiN waveguides, TSV, UCUT, etc.). The imec PDK under NDA will have the exact GDS layer mapping.

Reference numbers from literature:
  - Full active/passive silicon photonics: typically 12-20 mask levels
  - Passive-only: typically 8-10 mask levels
  - imec iSiPP50G full stack: approximately 17-20 layers including all implants
