Sources compiled from multiple IEEE research papers (2024-2025) on edge coupler performance.

SINGLE-TIP INVERSE TAPER (Standard 220 nm SOI):
- Coupling loss TE: ~1.9 dB (SMF-28)
- Coupling loss TM: ~4.0 dB (SMF-28)
- Key limitation: mode mismatch + substrate leakage

DOUBLE-TIP INVERSE TAPER (Wang et al., 2016):
- Coupling loss TE: 1.10 dB, TM: 1.52 dB
- Bandwidth: 1480-1630 nm
- 3-dB alignment tolerance: X ±1.25 μm, Y ±0.95 μm

MULTI-TIP (10-TIP, 2021):
- Coupling efficiency: -0.72 dB at 1310 nm, -1.12 dB at 1550 nm
- 1-dB bandwidth: >400 nm for both TE and TM
- PDL: <0.4 dB
- 1-dB horizontal misalignment tolerance: 2.35 μm

SiN-Si DUAL-STAGE WITH SWG (Jiang et al., 2024):
- TE coupling loss: 0.61 dB (simulated), TM: 0.95 dB
- PDL: <0.4 dB across 1500-1600 nm, <0.2 dB in C-band
- Bandwidth: 100 nm (1500-1600 nm)
- Device length: 315.8 μm
- No suspended structures, CUMEC-compatible foundry

SiN-ASSISTED EDGE COUPLER (Cheng, 2019):
- Coupling loss: 0.35 dB at 1570 nm (with index-matching oil, high-NA fiber)
- 1-dB bandwidth: 95 nm
- 1-dB alignment tolerance: ±2.5 μm horizontal, ±1.0 μm vertical, >25 μm axial

O-BAND BI-LEVEL TAPER (Yi et al., 2025, Opt. Lett. Vol 50 No 5):
- Coupling loss TE: 0.53-0.57 dB, TM: 0.45-0.55 dB (1260-1360 nm)
- Minimum measured loss: ~0.54 dB
- PDL: ~0.18 dB
- For 4 μm MFD fiber

SiN SPOT-SIZE CONVERTER (Brunetti, 2023):
- Coupling loss: 0.18 dB (lowest reported in C-band for SiN)
- Taper length: 600 μm
- Tip aspect ratio: 8:1 to 28:1

TRIPLE-TIP SSC (2020, Si3N4):
- Coupling loss: <1 dB over 600 nm window around 1550 nm
- Low PDL, polarization-insensitive

EDGE COUPLER ALIGNMENT TOLERANCE (from FAU research):
- Best reported 1-dB tolerance: ±3.1 μm
- Expanded beam 1-dB lateral tolerance: 6 μm
- Expanded beam 1-dB axial tolerance: >100 μm
- Rotational tolerance: 1.1°
- Direct coupling tolerance: 2.6-2.8 μm

FACET QUALITY:
- Adhesive in optical path reduces loss from ~3 dB to ~2.13 dB/coupler
- High-index lens materials preferred for turning mirrors (TIR preservation)
