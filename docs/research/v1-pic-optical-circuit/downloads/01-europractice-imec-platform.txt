--- iSiPP50G Platform — Process Modules ---

The platform is a fixed 130nm CMOS process technology with 3 silicon patterning steps at three etch depths in a 220nm Si device layer:

  - 70nm etch (shallow) — rib waveguides, grating couplers
  - 160nm etch (partial) — strip waveguides, tapers
  - 220nm etch (full, 193nm litho) — deep etch, full Si thickness, strip waveguides

Additional modules:
  - Gate oxide + Poly-Silicon: single etch at 160nm (193nm litho). Enables advanced grating couplers, poly-Si waveguide.
  - Ion implantation in Si: 8 implant levels (4x n-type, 4x p-type). Enables Si carrier depletion/injection/accumulation devices, Ge photodetectors, doped Si resistors.
  - Ge module: 100% Ge(Si) RPCVD selective epitaxial growth & 2 implant levels. Enables Ge photodetectors, Ge(Si) EA modulator.
  - Silicide tungsten contact: ohmic contacts to doped Si, standard CMOS contact plugs.
  - Metal heater: dedicated metal layer for heaters.
  - Two-level metal interconnect: Cu-based two-level metallization.
  - Aluminium passivation: Al finish metallization.
  - Deep trench: etched to expose edge coupler facets.

Waveguide types: strip and rib waveguides (from three etch depths), poly-Si waveguides. Exposed waveguide option available (oxide removed from top).

SOI substrate: 220nm top Si / 2000nm buried oxide (BOX) on Si handle wafer. Optional high-resistive Si substrate.

Metal stack: dedicated heater metal layer + 2-level Cu BEOL + Al passivation top metal.
